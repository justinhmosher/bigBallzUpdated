Home Run Derby

Facts

  • A team consists of three MLB players who are not all on the same team.
  • Each team costs $50 to buy.
  • You can buy a minimum of 1 team and a maximum of 7 teams.

Rules

  1. The game starts on Sunday - March 30th, 2025
  2. The goal is to choose at least one player to hit a home run per week.
    • Selection starts every Sunday 12:00am PST, and ends 11:59pm PST that same Sunday.
    • After Sunday, you may no longer change your team for the week.
  3. If one or more of your players hits a home run that week, you advance to the following week, else you are out.
  4. When a player hits a home run for your team, you may not select that same player the next week. However, the player will become available once again the week after.
  5. At any point, if a selected player hits a grand slam, you may not choose that player again for the rest of the tournament. You will still advance to the following week.
  6. If a tiebreaker is required, it will be decided by total cumulative home runs throughout the tournament.
