Home Run Derby Rules - Weekly Game

Basics

  • A team consists of ten MLB players. All ten players may not be on the same NFL roster.
  • Each team costs $30 to buy.
  • You can buy a minimum of 1 team and a maximum of 7 teams.

Rules

  1. The game starts on Sunday before any week of the MLB season.
  2. The goal is to have your ten selected players hit the most cumulative home runs.
    • You may choose/change player selections any time Sunday 12am PST through 11:59pm PST.
    • Starting Monday, 12:00am PST, you may no longer change your player selections.
  3. The user with the most cumulative home runs by the following Saturday wins the pot!
  4. Any home run hit on a Sunday will not be counted.
