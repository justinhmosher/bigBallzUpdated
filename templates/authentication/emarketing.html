Introducing the MLB Home Run Derby

Big news—we’re launching an all-new MLB Home Run Derby game! This game brings an exciting twist to fantasy baseball, and the best part? Anyone can join, and the prize pot is unlimited!

How It Works:

  • Pick 3 MLB players (max 2 per team)
  • Entry Fee: $50 per team (enter up to 7 teams!)
  • Objective: Each week, pick at least one player who hits a home run to stay in the game.
  • Player Strategy: Once a player homers for you, they’re unavailable the following week only(grand slams bench them for the season!)
  • Last one standing wins the pot!

We’ve updated our league structure so there’s no limit on the number of players, meaning bigger prize pools and even more competition!
