Eligibility

The Tournament is open to individuals who are at least 19 years of age at the time of registration and are legal residents of certain U.S. states or districts. Please see the detailed list for eligibility.

Tournament Entry

  • Team Composition: Each team consists of two (2) NFL players who must be either a WR, RB, or TE.
  • Team Limitations: Each participant is allowed to buy a minimum of one (1) team and a maximum of twenty (20) teams.
  • Selection Period: Team selections must be finalized by the start of Week 1 of the NFL season.

Tournament Rules and Structure

Buy-In

  • Entry Fee: To participate in the Fantasy Sports Tournament (the "Tournament"), each participant must pay a buy-in fee of $62.50. This fee must be paid before the commencement of the NFL season.

Eligibility

  • Participants: Must be at least 19 years of age at the time of registration and a legal resident of eligible U.S. states or districts.
  • Player Selection: Each participant must select two (2) National Football League (NFL) players to form their team. The selected players must be either a Tight End (TE), Running Back (RB), or Wide Receiver (WR).

Gameplay

  • At the beginning of Week 1, participants choose their two NFL players. To advance to the following week, at least one of the selected players must score a touchdown during their games in that week.
  • If neither of the selected players scores a touchdown in a week, the participant is eliminated from the Tournament.
  • The Tournament concludes at the end of Week 18 of the NFL season.

Prizes

  • The last participant remaining wins the entire pot. If there are multiple participants remaining at the end of Week 18, the pot is evenly split among those participants.
