The Chosen Rules

Team Composition

Team: Consists of two NFL players (Running Back, Wide Receiver, Tight End, or Quarter Back - rushing only) on separate NFL rosters.

  • Each team costs $50 to buy.
  • You can buy a minimum of 1 team and a maximum of 20 teams.

Definitions

User: A person who signs up for The Chosen and purchases 1 or more teams.

Rules

  1. The game starts on Tuesday, week 1 of the NFL season.
  2. On that Tuesday, you select two players per team to score a touchdown and one or more offensive yards.
  3. If one or both players score a touchdown and one or more offensive yards, then that team moves onto the following week.
  4. If both players DO NOT score a touchdown, then that team is eliminated.
    • You may choose/change player selections at any time Tuesday and Wednesday.
    • Starting Thursday, you may no longer choose/change players.
  5. Steps 1-4 are repeated until week 18.
  6. If a player scores a touchdown and one or more offensive yards for a team at any point in the season, you may no longer select that player to score FOR THAT TEAM ONLY.
    • You may still select that player to a touchdown for a different team.
  7. The user with the last team remaining wins the pot!
  8. If there are multiple teams in after week 18, then the pot is split amongst the users proportional to how many surviving teams they have.

Example

A user paid $250 for 5 teams. It is now week 7 of the NFL season and the user has 2 teams remaining, and the user selects the following players to score a touchdown and one or more offensive yards:
Team 1 = Travis Kelce or Tyreek Hill
Team 2 = Austin Eckler or Mike Evans
If Travis Kelce, Mike Evans, and Tyreek Hill score a touchdown and one or more offensive yards that week, then both teams stay alive!

Going Forward

Team 1 will no longer be able to select Travis Kelce, nor Tyreek Hill for the remainder of the season, and team 2 will no longer be able to select Mike Evans for the remainder of the season.
